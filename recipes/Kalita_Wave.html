Kalita Wave

Description

The Kalita Wave is the perfect percolator style coffee. It is easy to adjust the strength by adjusting the beans used.

  • 15 to 20 grams of coffee
  • JX-Pro grind at roughly 34 clicks
  • 300g of water
  • Filter compatible with your Kalita Wave
  1. Grind all of your beans
  2. Prewet your filter, we recommend using hot water while placed in the basket
  3. Place all of your beans inside the filter
  4. Slowly pour your water, watch your pour!
