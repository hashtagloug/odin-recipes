Cafelat Robot

Description

Using the Cafelat Robot, we are able to pull a manual espresso shot. It does require a lot of practice, but it is definitely a rewarding skill.

  • 17 grams of coffee beans
  • JX-Pro Grind at 14-17 clicks depending on bean
  • 60g of water
  • Expect about 38g of espresso
  1. Grind all the beans. Tip, as the beans get older you may need a finer grind.
  2. Boil your water
  3. Add beans to the basket
  4. Use distribution tool to spread out beans, then tamp!
  5. Add filter to the top, then add all the water
  6. Lock the basket, slowly start pulling and watch the pressure!
