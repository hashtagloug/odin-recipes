Vietnamese Phin

Description

The phin is a traditional way of preparing coffee. It's sweet, it's strong, and its very simple to make.

  • 14 grams of coffee
  • JX-Pro Grind anywhere between 14-17 clicks
  • Vietnamese Phin
  • Roughly 14 ounces of water
  1. Set up your cup
  2. Add plate to the top of your cup
  3. Add basket on top of cup, add coffee inside basket
  4. Slowly pour water and let bloom for 45 seconds
  5. Shake out the basket
